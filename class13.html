About Everless

In the kingdom of Sempera, time is currency—extracted from blood, bound to iron, and consumed to add time to one’s own lifespan. The rich aristocracy, like the Gerlings, tax the poor to the hilt, extending their own lives by centuries. No one resents the Gerlings more than Jules Ember. A decade ago, she and her father were servants at Everless, the Gerlings’ palatial estate, until a fateful accident forced them to flee in the dead of night. When Jules discovers that her father is dying, she knows that she must return to Everless to earn more time for him before she loses him forever. But going back to Everless brings more danger—and temptation—than Jules could have ever imagined. Soon she’s caught in a tangle of violent secrets and finds her heart torn between two people she thought she’d never see again. Her decisions have the power to change her fate—and the fate of time itself.



Characters

Jules Emeber Jules is the protagonist of the story. She is 17 years old and saves her town by going against the Gerlings and the Queen.
Ina Gold Ina is the princess of Sempera. She is also Jules' sister and is thought to have powers.
Roan Gerling Roan is the son of the Gerlings. He is the one who helps Jules at first but then back stabs her later on.
Liam Gerling Liam is also the son of the Gerlings and starts of as one of the main antagonists in the book but later helps Jules escape the palace.
The Queen The Queen is the main antagonist of the book and is later revealed to be the sorceress. She steals peoples time.



Series

Everless is a fantasy novel set in the future where time is a currency. In the first book we are introduced to the characters and Jules who travels to the Gerling castle to put an end to this currency which is causing war and sickness in her town.

Everless

Evermore is the second and last book of the series. After discovering shocking secrets about the Queen and herself, Jules takes the help of Liam Gerling to stop the war.
